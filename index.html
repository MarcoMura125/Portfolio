About me

I’m a Master’s student in Electronics Engineering at the “Enzo Ferrari” Department of Engineering, University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, with hands-on experience in hardware design and embedded firmware development. My background focuses on IoT systems and PCB design, and I’m particularly interested in millimeter-wave RADAR technology and neuromorphic computing. Recent projects I’ve worked on include implementing the LISTA algorithm for Multidimensional Harmonic Retrieval and exploring RRAM-based Analog Matrix Computing. I’m currently developing my master’s thesis on vital-sign estimation using millimeter-wave RADAR. I’ve also been part of the UNIMORE Formula Student Driverless team, where I contributed to the Hardware Design division.

Projects

Here you can find information about my projects. Feel free to explore and contact me for any inquiries at muramarco99@gmail.com

LISTA-Toeplitz for Multidimensional Harmonic Retrieval

Reproduction of the numerical experiments described in the paper “Structured LISTA for Multidimensional Harmonic Retrieval” by Rong Fu, Yimin Liu, Tianyao Huang, and Yonina C. Eldar, published in 2021.

RRAM-Based Analog Circuit for Matrix Inversion: Simulation and Analysis

Simulation of the RRAM-based analog matrix inversion circuit described in “Invited Tutorial: Analog Matrix Computing With Crosspoint Resistive Memory Arrays” (Sun & Ielmini, 2022).

Quadcopter Electronic Speed Controller PCB Design

Design of a PCB for an Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) intended to drive a brushless DC motor, specifically for multirotor applications.

Human Tracking and Fall Detection Using FMCW Radar

Integration of commercial FMCW radar modules into a custom PCB, with testing and demonstration of human tracking and fall detection capabilities.

Derivation of the Electrochemical Impedance for Linear Semi-Infinite Diffusion

Derivation a linearized model that describes the electrochemical impedance associated with charge transfer and mass transport phenomena occurring at faradaic electrodes.
