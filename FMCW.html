RRAM-Based Analog Circuit for Matrix Inversion: Simulation and Analysis

During my internship at Techboard Group, I had the opportunity to work for six months on mmWave FMCW RADAR systems. In particular, I experimented with commercial RADAR modules for People Tracking and Fall Detection applications. My work focused on the design of PCB carrier boards for integrating RADAR modules into embedded systems based on ESP32 microcontrollers, as well as on the development of data visualization interfaces.
