Quadcopter Electronic Speed Controller PCB Design

Here’s the design of a PCB for an Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) intended to drive a brushless DC motor, specifically for multirotor applications.

This work is part of a larger group project, in which my colleagues and I designed both the PCB and the firmware for a quadcopter ESC. Since my main contribution focused on the hardware design, this section presents only the aspects related to the ESC PCB.

In a drone ESC PCB, the main design constraints are clear: it must be small, lightweight, and capable of handling high currents. Meeting all these requirements at once is quite challenging, but our final result was very satisfying: a compact PCB measuring just 50×25 mm.
